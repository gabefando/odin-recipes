Tiramisu

Description

Here is an easy to make Tiramisu recipe that will be done in under 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • CoffeeEspresso
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder

Instructions

  1. Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together untill stiff peaks.
  2. Dip ladyfingers in coffee (only a quick dip on both sides).
  3. Smooth a layer of the creamy filling over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat step 3 until you have reached the top of your container and dust with
    cocoa powder.
